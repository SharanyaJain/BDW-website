This is an application for managing the check in/check out of materials at the BDW Material Circulation Station. 

On the Home page, you can navigate to check in/check out forms for any of the available materials by clicking on the icons. Materials with quantities >5 units, they will be highlighted in green (sufficient quantity), materials with units < 5 but > 1 are in orange (moderate quantity), and < 1 in red (limited quantity). This can help simplify restocking. 

There is also a light/dark mode toggle for added accessibility. 

You can also access the transaction history using the History button that displays the timestamp, name, email, material and quantity checked in/checked out. 

In the form, the material checked in or checked out by the user updates the amount available in real time. The form will not let you check out a higher quantity of material than is available in the inventory. 

The admin view allows you to update the inventory and add new materials. Users cannot navigate to this page from the home page. It can only be accessed by navigating to /admin in the URL manually. 

In the admin view, you will also have the option to download QR codes for any material that is generated. By clicking on 'Download All', you will get a PDF will all the available QR codes in one go for easy printing.
